Self-Care Practices

Simple techniques you can use daily to maintain energetic balance:

  • Grounding: Connect with the earth through barefoot walking or visualization
  • Meditation: Even 5-10 minutes daily can create profound shifts
  • Journaling: Process emotions and insights through written reflection
  • Energy Clearing: Use sage, sound, or intention to clear your space
  • Breathwork: Simple breathing exercises to center and calm your energy
